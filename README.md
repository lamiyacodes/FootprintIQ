# FootprintIQ 🌱

**Know your carbon. Own your impact.**

A carbon footprint awareness platform that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their environmental impact through simple inputs and personalized insights.

🔗 **Live Demo:** [footprint-iq.vercel.app](https://footprint-iq.vercel.app)

## Features

- Carbon footprint calculator across transport, food and energy
- Real-time comparison with India average, global average and Paris Agreement target
- Carbon equivalents — understand your score in relatable terms
- Personalized 7-day action plan based on your highest emission category
- Shareable score card with your personal carbon stats

## Tech Stack

- HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- Vercel Serverless Functions
- Deployed on Vercel
